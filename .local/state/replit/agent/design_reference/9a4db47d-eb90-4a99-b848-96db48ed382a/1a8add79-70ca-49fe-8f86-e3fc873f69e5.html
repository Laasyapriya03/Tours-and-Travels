Heritage & Historical Places

Neuschwanstein Castle

A 19th-century Romanesque Revival palace that inspired Disney's Sleeping Beauty Castle. Perched on a hill in Bavaria, Germany, this fairy-tale castle offers breathtaking views and rich history.

Built: 1869-1886

The Parthenon

An ancient temple dedicated to the goddess Athena, located on the Acropolis in Athens, Greece. This masterpiece of ancient Greek architecture represents the pinnacle of classical civilization.

Built: 447-432 BC

Chichen Itza

One of the New Seven Wonders of the World, this ancient Mayan city in Mexico features the famous El Castillo pyramid. A testament to the advanced astronomical and mathematical knowledge of the Maya.

Built: 600-1200 AD

Taj Mahal

An ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan as a tomb for his wife. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world.

Built: 1632-1648
